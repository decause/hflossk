OLPC  Quiz Rating Rubric                                                                 January 14, 2010

 

Full Credit (90-100%):  A thoughtful essay which demonstrates a thorough understanding of the materials discussed.  Discussion includes significant details from within and without reading.

 

Quality Response (80-89%):  Essay demonstrates a competent grasp of essential concepts in materials under discussion. Inclusion of significant details/examples from reading.

 

Average Response (70-79%):  Essay demonstrates a rudimentary grasp of essential concepts under discussion.  Sparse inclusion of detail from articles.

 

Below Average Response (65-69%):  Essay demonstrates superficial grasp of essential concepts under discussion.  Few or no details from articles.

 

Unacceptable Response (0-64%):  Essay demonstrates poor understanding or misapprehension of essential concepts under discussion.  Either no or irrelevant examples used in support of discussion.
